1.3. Downloading the ISO

In order to download the ISO image, you must be registered in the redBorder Live web.
Once you are logged in to the redborder Live web, you must click on Downloads. On this page you will find all of the redborder editions along with their corresponding specifications and characteristics.
To download the redborder Flowgate (client proxy), click in the corresponding download button.
